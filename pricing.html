FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do reviewers need a paid seat?

No. You only pay for members of your team. Clients and external reviewers comment for free via a share link — no account required.

What counts as a "seat"?

A seat is one teammate inside your workspace who can create projects and manage reviews. External reviewers are always free and unlimited.

Can I switch between monthly and annual?

Anytime. Annual saves 20%. If you switch to monthly mid-term, the change applies at your next renewal.

Is my unreleased footage secure?

Yes — SOC 2 Type II infrastructure, granular access, and forensic, session-level watermarking on Studio and above.

What happens when I hit my storage limit?

Nothing breaks. You can archive finished projects or upgrade; we'll never delete your work or block playback without warning.
